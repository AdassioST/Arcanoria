#spellweaving

**Incantation:** *Control is Mastery, Emotion is Fuel, [[Consciousness]] is Magic.*

A popular statement and magic principle which defines the seven bindings of the [[Auric Heptacode]] in [[The Principles of Magic]], often used to determine the perfect [[Spellweaver]] by having [[Spellweaving ]]mastery in three primary categories: [[Mastery Over Chaos]], [[Potential of Creation]], and [[Key of Attunement]].

It's one of the core virtues that [[Perfect Reflection]] sought after.
