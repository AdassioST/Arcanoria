#technology

A side effect of broken [[Coherence]] that manifests as chaotic vibrations that rend the interwoven strings of the [[Great Harmonic Loom]] and contaminate magical structure with [[Primal White Noise]].
 
The most common type of [[Discordant Interference]] comes from incorrectly following [[The Principles of Magic]] and having [[Spellweaving]] backfiring with random unpredictable outcomes due to the [[Primal White Noise]].
