#spellweaving 

Memory + Time

Creative use cases
- Pictures of the past (Detective Magic).
- Using the past for blueprints on how to rebuild something.
- Cloning vats of [[Strand]], [[Luminance]], [[Flux]]. Electrified time water healing that recreates tissue based on the blueprint of tissue ([[Strand]]), in a healing carrier medium ([[Flux]]) and electrical surges ([[Luminance]]).
- Altering fields of the memory pictures ([[Strand]]) through illusory magic manipulation [[Luminance]]) for covering crime scenes.
- Detective magic vs Criminal magic.
