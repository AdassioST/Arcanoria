[[Soul Leitmotif]]: [[Crystal]]

Tempo: Accelerando

**Effect:** Combined [[Dance]] movement with dual chakras to jump in the sky as a [[Crystal]]-edged flame whirl where landing creates a row of crystal spikes filled with [[Void]].

Root: [[Crystal]], [[Major Note]] - Hollow throwable [[Crystal]] shuriken.
