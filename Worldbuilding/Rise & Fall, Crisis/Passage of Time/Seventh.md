Description: *The minimum unit of time tracking.*

This is the minimum unit of time tracking in the game, equivalent to a Day. It accounts for 3 real life minutes. It is called a [[Seventh]] due to the thematical nature of the [[Auric Heptacode]]. It allows for time tracking similar to the Aztec Solar calendar by utilizing the numbers, 3, 4 and 7 to create the whole system. A [[Phase]] is composed of 21 [[Seventh]] precisely due to being the result of 3 x 7 = 21.

Due to the powerful natural [[Resonance]] of the number 21 through its numerological meaning. Every [[Phase]] has a special type of [[Seventh]] where [[Attunement for Magic]] is at its peak called [[Ritual Seventh]] only on the 21th [[Seventh]].
