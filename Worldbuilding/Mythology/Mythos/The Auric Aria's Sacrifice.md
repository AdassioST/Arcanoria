The mortal death of the [[Auric Aria]]. A blasphemous, [[Inescapable Truth]] of the creation of magic detailed in the myth of the [[Eighth Cycle]].

It says that [[The Silent Expanse]] came to be from her place of death with its epicenter being a moonlit crescent fields of [[Fated Flower]]s turned into [[Forsaken Flower]]s after the release of her [[Golden Ichor]] into the waves below.
