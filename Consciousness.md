#spellweaving

*The ink which writes [[The Eternal Symphony]]*.

An abstract form of raw existence, it is one of the most essential concepts for giving form into matter and energy from chaos, thus [[Mastery Over Chaos]] being the principal method of transforming reality. 

It exists in all of [[The Infinite Void]], and emerges as a variation of it as a structured deterministic pattern in [[The First Overtone]]. Ironically, it is a diluted version of [[Primal White Noise]] that has stabilized into existence.

Crystalized [[Consciousness]] is the primary element of [[Crystal]] and reflects this as the laws of quantum collapse in the [[Auric Heptacode]].
